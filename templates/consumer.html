HOW POOLS WORK
🤝 Community energy pools let multiple consumers join together to reach bulk purchase thresholds.
⚡ When a pool hits 50 kWh, all members receive an 8% discount automatically.
🔒 Payments are held in escrow until the pool fills and energy is delivered.
📊 Each pool transaction is recorded on the P2P ledger with a SHA-256 hash.
